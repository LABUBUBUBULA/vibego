Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2026

1. Overview

VibeGo is a voice-based social networking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data when using VibeGo services.

2. Information You Provide

When you create an account or use VibeGo, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address, display name, password, avatar, gender, birthday, country, interests, and profile bio
  • User-generated content: chat messages, voice room interactions, forum posts, comments, and profile information
  • Settings and preferences you configure within the app

3. Information Collected Automatically

When you use VibeGo, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App usage data: features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app stability
  • Push notification tokens for delivering notifications

4. Device Permissions

VibeGo may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: Required for voice chat and voice room participation
  • Camera: Used for taking and updating profile photos
  • Photo Library: Used for selecting images and media to share
  • Location: Used to provide localized content and connect you with nearby users (only when enabled)
  • Push Notifications: Used to send messages, social updates, and activity alerts
  • App Tracking Transparency: We may request permission to improve your experience. You can decline this request without affecting app functionality.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain VibeGo services including voice rooms, messaging, and social features
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Process in-app purchases and virtual item transactions
  • Send notifications about messages, social activity, and service updates
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our terms

6. Payments

In-app purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. VibeGo does not directly collect or store your payment card details. We may receive transaction identifiers, product information, and purchase status to fulfill virtual item delivery and verify transactions.

7. Third-Party Services

VibeGo does not share your personal data with third-party analytics or advertising platforms. We may use the Apple App Store for purchase processing, which operates under Apple's own privacy policy.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating VibeGo
  • App Store platforms for purchase processing
  • Law enforcement or legal authorities when required by law
  • Safety partners to protect users from harm

9. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit and at rest. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

10. Your Rights and Choices

You may:

  • Edit or update your profile information at any time
  • Control device permissions through your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings
  • Decline App Tracking Transparency requests
  • Request account deletion through app settings, which will remove your account data
  • Log out of your account at any time

11. Children's Privacy

VibeGo is not intended for children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided personal information, we will take steps to delete such data.

12. International Users

VibeGo is available in multiple countries. By using the app, you consent to your data being processed in the country where our servers are located, which may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the effective date. Continued use of VibeGo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
