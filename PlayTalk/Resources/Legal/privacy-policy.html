Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

1. Overview

PlayTalk is a game voice social app. This Privacy Policy explains how profile, chat, room, forum, gift, and app activity data may be used to provide PlayTalk services.

2. Information You Provide

We may collect account information such as email, display name, password credential state, avatar, gender, birthday, country, interests, profile bio, and settings choices.

3. Social and Room Data

We may process voice room membership, room creation data, room collection records, browsing history, forum posts, comments, likes, follows, private chat metadata, gifts, coins, levels, and growth values.

4. Device and Usage Data

We may process basic device, app version, crash, diagnostics, and usage information to keep PlayTalk secure, stable, and improved.

5. How We Use Data

Data is used to sign you in, show your profile, recommend rooms, render chats and forums, maintain follows and gifts, provide customer support, prevent abuse, and improve app quality.

6. Payments

If purchases are enabled, payment is handled by the App Store. PlayTalk may receive transaction status, product identifiers, and purchase results needed to unlock virtual items.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers, app stores, legal authorities, or safety partners when needed to operate the app, process purchases, comply with law, or protect users.

8. Data Storage

This preview build may store mock and local session data on device. Production versions may store account, room, message, and purchase-related data on servers.

9. Your Choices

You can edit profile information, log out, or request account deletion from Settings. Deleting an account may remove local session data and, in production, request removal of server-side account data.

10. Children

PlayTalk is not intended for children under the minimum age required by local law. Users who are not old enough should not use the app.

11. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data, but no internet or mobile service can be guaranteed completely secure.

12. Updates

We may update this policy when features or legal requirements change. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or account requests, contact PlayTalk support through the app settings or customer service entry.
