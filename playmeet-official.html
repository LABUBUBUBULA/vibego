About PlayMeet

PlayMeet is a social networking app focused on live voice rooms and friendly online interaction. Users can join chat rooms, send messages, share posts, customize profiles, and use virtual coins for in-app gifts.

Voice Rooms

Join real-time voice rooms and talk with people who share your interests.

Messaging

Send private messages and stay connected with friends inside the app.

Community

Post moments, view discussions, and report inappropriate content when needed.

Safety

  • Users can report chats and posts.
  • Users can delete chats and manage account settings.
  • Users can delete their account from Settings.
  • PlayMeet is intended for users aged 17 and above.
